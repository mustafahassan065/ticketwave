How Ticket
Reselling Works

Anyone can do this. You need to work systematically, learn the platform, and trust the Ticketwave process. Here's the exact flow.

01
Monitor for Drops & Restocks

Ticketwave's custom monitors scan 14+ European ticketing platforms 24/7. The moment tickets drop or restocks appear, alerts fire to your Discord and phone via the app. You're notified before the general public even knows tickets are available.

02
Read the Team's Event Analysis

Every day, the Ticketwave team posts detailed Discord analyses breaking down which events are worth targeting — specific dates, sections, platforms, expected resale margins, and the exact strategy. You never need to research blind.

03
Buy Tickets at Face Value

Using the monitors and app, you buy tickets the moment they become available — at face value. The Queue.it integration helps you beat the crowd. Start with as little as €50, though €400 is recommended for comfortable scaling across multiple opportunities.

04
List & Sell on Secondary Markets

Post your tickets on secondary platforms at market price. Ticketwave guides members on optimal listing strategies, timing, and pricing. The secondary market for in-demand events is deep — demand always outstrips supply for the best events.

05
Collect Your Profit & Repeat

Funds clear, profit is booked, and you're ready for the next wave. The Ticketwave community shares wins in Discord — keeping everyone motivated and showing what's possible. Worst case scenario: you sell at face value and break even. Best case: thousands of euros per event.
